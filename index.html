One of the club I've been involved in is Katipunan. It is a cultural club that teaches students about the Philippine culture. We involve ourselves in the food, music, traditionals, and people that bring the cultue alive. We are able to participate in a perfermance night where we display different Philippine folk dances with a storyline that allows the audience to learn and enjoy the theatrical experience. I've participated in one competition held in Redondo Beach. Before that competition, we participated in a fundraiser to raise money for our fees and costumes.
